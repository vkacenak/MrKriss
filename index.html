My style

I love to give a new meaning to everything people see and use every day. To look at things not by they purpose, but by their looks and through that work my way until I can make something completely different out of it. Once you start thinking out of the box even in the little terms, whole world opens up to you and incredible things happen.

Who am I?

I am a student and self-taught artist from Prague, currently studying in Berlin.​

Throughout the years, I’ve realized that even the most ordinary things we use daily in our lives can be somehow unique.
